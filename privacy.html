Privacy
Policy

Last updated — March 17, 2026

01 — What We Collect

Zuki does not store any user data persistently. When you use a command, the Bot temporarily processes the following to fulfill your request:

  • Your Discord user ID (to respond to your interaction)
  • Any file URL or attachment you provide (image, audio, or video)

None of this information is saved to a database or retained after the interaction is complete.

02 — How It's Used

Data is used solely to process your request in real time — analyzing file metadata for images, audio, and video, or converting an image to GIF format — and is discarded immediately after the response is sent.

03 — Third Parties

Zuki uses the following third-party services:

  • Discord API — to receive and respond to interactions
  • sharp — for local image processing (no data is sent externally)
  • ffmpeg / ffprobe — for local audio and video processing (no data is sent externally)

No data is sold or shared with any third party.

04 — Data Deletion

Since no data is stored, there is nothing to delete. If you have any concerns, you are welcome to reach out via Discord.

05 — Children's Privacy

Zuki does not knowingly collect data from anyone. Since no data is stored, there is no special risk to any age group.

06 — Changes

This policy may be updated at any time. Continued use of the Bot constitutes acceptance of any changes.
